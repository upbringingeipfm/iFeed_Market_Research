Services · Custom

Bespoke research, Tier C moat embedded.

When a published report does not answer your specific question, iFeed scopes a custom engagement — always delivered with the same zero-hallucination discipline and Governance Architect lens.

What we scope

  • Market-entry studies — regulatory landscape, competitor deep-dive, buyer journey, go-to-market recommendation
  • Competitive positioning — vendor benchmarking, capability gap analysis, differentiation roadmap
  • M&A target identification — sector mapping, acquisition candidate profiles, synergy mapping
  • Technology landscape reviews — emerging tech scan, patent mapping, build-vs-buy analysis
  • Regulatory gap assessments — QMSR readiness, ICH M10 transition audit, E6(R3) gap review
  • Pricing benchmark studies — RFP tear-downs, published-price surveys, negotiation anchoring
  • Financial modelling — market sizing, penetration models, investment NPV
  • Training needs assessments — capability-gap mapping, curriculum design

Process

  1. Initial discovery call with Sunita · scope, audience, decision being served
  2. Written scope document and fixed-price proposal · 3–5 business days
  3. Signed SOW · project kickoff
  4. Fortnightly checkpoint calls · mid-project draft review · final delivery
