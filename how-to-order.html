Ordering a report or engagement.

Three ways to engage with iFeed — licensed report, custom research, or consulting. Each has a defined ordering flow.

1. License a published report

  1. Download free sample via the Request Sample form — receive within 1 business day
  2. Choose licensing tier — Single-User / Corporate / Enterprise
  3. Place order via email to upbringing.eipfm@outlook.com with purchase order reference or credit-card processing preference
  4. Receive report — full HTML, PDF, and Markdown formats delivered within 1 business day of payment
  5. Quarterly refresh — automated delta updates for 12 months post-purchase

2. Commission custom research

  1. Initial scoping call with Sunita · 30 minutes · complimentary
  2. Written scope document and fixed-price proposal · 3–5 business days
  3. Signed SOW and first invoice (30%) · project kickoff
  4. Fortnightly checkpoint calls during execution
  5. Mid-project draft review at 50% completion
  6. Final delivery with client Q&A session · balance invoice
  7. 30-day follow-up window included

3. Engage for consulting

  1. Discovery call with Sunita · 30 minutes · complimentary
  2. Scoping document mapping your question to our catalogue · 2–3 business days
  3. Signed engagement letter · project kickoff within 1 week
  4. Deliverable cadence per engagement type (see Consulting page)
  5. Final readout and Q&A session
  6. 30-day follow-up window included

Payment terms

  • Corporate purchase orders accepted
  • Credit card via invoice payment portal
  • Wire transfer (EUR, On request GBP accepted)
  • Standard terms: 30% on signed SOW, balance on delivery
  • For subscription / Horizon: annual invoice, NET 30

Refund policy

Reports: 7-day satisfaction guarantee from delivery. Custom research: scope-change protocol in SOW. Consulting: milestone-based payments, milestones explicit in engagement letter.
