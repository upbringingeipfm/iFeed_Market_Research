24. Methodology

This report follows the canonical 30-section iFeed MRA structure, authored with zero-hallucination discipline.

  • Primary sources: published regulations (cited by section), SEC EDGAR filings for listed vendors, vendor product documentation, regulatory-agency databases (FDA 483, EMA inspection reports, ClinicalTrials.gov), academic literature (PubMed, SSRN).
  • Secondary sources: Grand View Research, MarketsandMarkets, Mordor Intelligence cross-triangulation where their publications touch this domain. All synthesised sources cited inline.
  • Tertiary / triangulation: where primary sources are unavailable for a figure, triangulation uses public vendor metrics + industry-association benchmarks. Every triangulated figure flagged.
  • Practitioner authorship: Sections 16–22 authored directly by Sunita Nawale, Regulatory Quality Governance Architect, from 20 years field experience. Field observations anonymised.
  • Zero-hallucination discipline: every factual claim has a citation or explicit [VERIFY] / [FETCH] / [SUNITA] flag. Nothing plausibility-filled. See MRA Thorough Build Plan v1 §6 for full citation protocol.
